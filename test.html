Selected projects

Predictive Modeling of COVID-19 Spread using Artificial Neural Networks

I successfully used Artificial Neural Networks in RapidMiner to predict COVID-19 spread, optimizing models and employing a Multilayer Perceptron for time series analysis. The project showcased my AI expertise in addressing real-world challenges.

Text Mining and Opinion Classification in Hotel Reviews

In our Data Mining and AI project using RapidMiner, we analyzed 18,172 Andalusian hotel reviews, predicting ratings from preprocessed text. Classifiers were built and evaluated with cross-validation, exploring n-grams and word embeddings.

Optimizing Car Recommendations for Market Segments

My group used MATLAB's Fuzzy Logic Toolbox to create an expert system for car selection based on market preferences. We defined fuzzy sets for input variables and recommendation levels, utilizing real-world car data from https://coches.rastreator.com.
